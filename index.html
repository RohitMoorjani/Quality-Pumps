Welcome to Quality Pumps

QUALITY PUMPS PVT. LTD. is India’s leading manufacturer & sustainable water solutions provider. Quality Pumps Private Limited was established in 1991. We are specialized in manufacturing of various types of deepwell hand pump such as India Mark II, Mark III (VLOM). India Mark II & III with force lift attadrments. We also offer a variety of products like PVC pipes, Garden Pipes, Casing Pipes and Water Tanks. We are accredited with ISO 9001-2008 certificate.We are the regular award acheiving unit from last 25 years, We won the award for best SSI unit in India by goverment of India in 1999.
